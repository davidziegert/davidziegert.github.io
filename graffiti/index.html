Hello everyone!

At this point I would like to present my collection of street art photos.

These photos were taken between 2018 and 2022 while traveling through Thailand.

Taken in places around Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Nakhon Ratchasima, Udon Thani, Phetchaburi and many more.

Including pictures of one of Thailand's most famous street artists "Patcharapol Tangruen", also known as "Alex Face" and others!
